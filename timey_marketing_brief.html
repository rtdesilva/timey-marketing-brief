01

Product Overview

Timey is a physical productivity device designed to help users manage time and workflows without relying on screens. It transforms digital productivity tasks into tactile, physical actions using programmable mechanical keys, allowing users to trigger commands, timers, and macros instantly.

By combining macro automation, time tracking, and interactive lighting, Timey helps users stay focused while reducing phone and screen dependency.

The product is compact, customizable, and designed for modern digital workspaces such as study desks, creative studios, and remote work setups.

06

Consumer Insight

Many people rely on phones or software timers to manage their time and tasks. However, these digital tools often create distractions due to notifications, apps, and constant screen exposure.

Users want a simpler, faster, and more tactile way to control their time and productivity without distractions.
